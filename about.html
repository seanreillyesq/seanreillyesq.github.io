I'm a digital marketing leader, entrepreneur, and investor based in the UK. I've spent over 15 years building and scaling digital businesses across luxury hospitality, luxury goods, ecommerce, and SaaS.

Currently, I'm Head of Digital Marketing at Hand Picked Hotels, a £100m luxury hotel group. I lead digital strategy across performance marketing, CRM and loyalty, web development, and analytics.

Alongside that, I run several ventures:

  • CanMarket - my marketing consultancy, delivering integrated campaigns and project management for clients ranging from luxury brands to global corporations.
  • CanHost - high-performance web hosting for small businesses and specialist sites.
  • Email Marketing Tools - SaaS tools I co-founded, including Email Heatmaps and Link Shortener.
  • Run and Race - a running coaching business I co-direct.
  • CanInvest - my investment vehicle, building a portfolio of property and stocks focused on generating passive income.
  • Digibury - a community event series I organise for digital and creative professionals in Canterbury.

Before Hand Picked Hotels, I was:

  • Head of Ecommerce at Authentic Bespoke
  • Digital Marketing Manager at Watchfinder
  • Head of Marketing at ExtraDigital
  • Marketing Manager at View City Guides

My core expertise is in performance marketing, CRM and loyalty, SEO, paid media, web analytics, and martech architecture. I'm particularly interested in building scalable, cash-generative businesses.

Outside of work, I'm a husband, father of two, and local Scout leader. I spend my spare time on strength training, aviation and flight simulation, sailing, and tinkering with digital privacy and cybersecurity.

Sean Reilly is Head of Digital Marketing at Hand Picked Hotels, a £100m luxury hotel group, where he leads digital strategy across performance marketing, CRM, web development, and analytics.
