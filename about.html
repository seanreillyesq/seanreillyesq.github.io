Sean Reilly is a UK-based digital marketing leader, entrepreneur, and investor with a track record of delivering data-driven growth across luxury hospitality, luxury goods, ecommerce, SaaS, and digital businesses.

He is currently Head of Digital Marketing at Hand Picked Hotels, a £100m luxury hotel group, where he leads digital strategy across performance marketing, CRM & loyalty, web development, and analytics.

Alongside his corporate role, Sean runs multiple ventures:

  • Founder and Marketing Consultant at CanMarket Limited — integrated marketing campaigns, project management, and marketing consultancy services.
  • Founder and Director of CanHost — providing high-performance web hosting services for small businesses and specialist sites.
  • Co-founder & Marketing Director of Email Marketing Tools Limited — SaaS tools and content-driven properties including Email Heatmaps and Link Shortener.
  • Co-director of Run and Race Ltd — a running coaching business.
  • Founder of CanInvest Limited — building a diversified portfolio of property and stock investments, targeting £120k in annual income within 10 years.
  • Organiser of Digibury — a community event series for digital and creative professionals in Canterbury.

Previously, Sean served as:

  • Head of Ecommerce at Authentic Bespoke
  • Digital Marketing Manager at Watchfinder
  • Head of Marketing at ExtraDigital (rebranding from Cornish Web Services)
  • Marketing Manager at View City Guides

Sean brings deep expertise in performance marketing, CRM & loyalty, SEO, paid media, web analytics, and martech architecture. He is passionate about building scalable, cash-generative businesses.

He is also a husband, father of two, and local Scout leader. Outside of work, Sean enjoys strength training, aviation and flight simulation, sailing, and digital privacy & cybersecurity.
