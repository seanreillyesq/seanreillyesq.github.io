I'm a digital marketing leader, entrepreneur, and investor based in the UK. I've spent over 18 years building and scaling digital businesses across luxury hospitality, luxury goods, ecommerce, and SaaS.

I run CanMarket, a marketing consultancy helping brands with digital strategy, performance marketing, and martech. Alongside that, I run several other ventures:

  • CanHost - high-performance web hosting for small businesses and specialist sites.
  • Email Marketing Tools - SaaS tools I co-founded, including Email Heatmaps and Link Shortener.
  • Run and Race - a running coaching business I co-direct.
  • CanInvest - my personal investment holding company.
  • Digibury - a community event series I organise for digital and creative professionals in Canterbury.

Previously, I was:

  • Head of Digital Marketing at Hand Picked Hotels
  • Head of Ecommerce at Authentic Bespoke
  • Digital Marketing Manager at Watchfinder
  • Head of Marketing at ExtraDigital
  • Marketing Manager at View City Guides

My core expertise is in digital strategy, performance marketing, CRM and loyalty, SEO, paid media, web analytics, and martech architecture. I'm particularly interested in building scalable, cash-generative businesses.

Outside of work, I'm a husband, father of two, and local Scout leader. I spend my spare time on strength training, aviation and flight simulation, sailing, and tinkering with digital privacy and cybersecurity.

Sean Reilly is a digital marketing consultant and founder of CanMarket, helping brands with digital strategy, performance marketing, and martech. He is also co-founder of Email Marketing Tools, a SaaS business. With over 18 years' experience in digital marketing across luxury hospitality, ecommerce, and SaaS, Sean has held senior digital roles at Hand Picked Hotels and Watchfinder, and won multiple industry awards including the DMA Silver Award for Best Use of Programmatic and the Online Retail Award for Jewellery and Watches.
